# About

I'm a freelance developer with several years of experience designing software. What drives me is building things that last — readable codebases, well-tested, designed to evolve without pain.

## How I work

I practise *craft* development: I care about architecture, tests, and readability as much as features. I work in TypeScript, with a preference for domain-oriented architectures and short feedback loops.

In recent years I've been exploring AI applied to development — not as a substitute for judgment, but as an amplification tool. I use and evaluate models, agents, and integrations that genuinely change how software is designed.

I'm available for development, code audit, or technical mentoring missions. If you have a project that fits this profile, [get in touch](mailto:hello@example.com).
