# Now

*Updated April 2026.*

## What I'm working on

I'm currently building a personal tool for automating a technology watch — source aggregation, LLM summarisation, publication to this site. The goal is to reduce friction between "I read something interesting" and "it's published".

I'm also on a freelance mission on a B2B SaaS platform: progressive refactoring towards a domain-oriented architecture, setting up integration tests, and moving to strict TypeScript.

## What I'm learning

I'm digging into LLM agents in production — not demos, but real patterns that hold under load: error handling, observability, tool-call limits. Reading a lot, breaking a lot.

I'm also revisiting the fundamentals of design systems: typography, spacing, visual hierarchy. This site is the practice ground.

- *The Pragmatic Programmer* — Hunt & Thomas (always relevant)
